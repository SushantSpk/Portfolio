Process

How I approach every build.

A clear, repeatable method that reduces risk and ships better systems faster.

01

Understand the Problem

Before writing a single line of code, I dig into the real problem — the workflows, the bottlenecks, and what a successful outcome looks like.

02

Design the System

I map out the architecture, user flows, and integration points before building. The right design upfront saves weeks of rework later.

03

Build and Integrate

Clean, modular code. Each component is built to be tested independently and integrated smoothly into the larger system.

04

Test, Improve, and Ship

I test in real conditions, gather feedback, and iterate until the system is solid enough to trust in production.
